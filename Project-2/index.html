Recipe For Peanut Butter Chicken

"One chicken breast is enough, for one hungry person."

Where to Start:

        Use a wok.

Time Estimation:

  • Prep Time: 5-7 mins
  • Cook Time: 28-30 mins
  • Total Time: 38-40 mins

Ingredients:

  • Steam one bowl of rice
  • Peanut oil
  • 6-8 slices of ginger
  • Half a glove of garlic, minced
  • 1 boneless chicken breast
  • 1 tablespoon of peanut butter
  • Extra chopped peanuts (optional)
  • 1/2 cup of soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon of seasame oil
  • Splash of cooking wine
  • Salt
  • Splash of light vinegar
  • Cilantro
    • Tables
    A Picture to Reference: Steps:
    Prepare the rice. Rice steamer will take about 15-20 minutes depending on how much you are serving per person.
    Cut 6-8 slices of garlic. Cut half a glove of garlic, minced. Cut chicken breast into small pieces. Put stove on medium high. Add a small splash of peanut oil. Wait 30 seconds for the wok to come to warm temperature. Add ginger and garlic. Stir and flavor the ginger and garlic for 30 seconds, then add the chicken. Reduce heat if necessary.
    Stir and cook chicken breast for 5-7 minutes or until chicken is brown.
    Add a splash of peanut oil, flavor it with salt. Add splash of vinegar and cooking wine. Stir well.
    Add tablespoon of peanut butter. Add water. Cover for 5 minutes. Reduce temperature.
    Remove cover. Add soy sauce. Stir, then add soy sauce. Cook one minute. Serve with rice.
    Ready to serve.
